Welcome to DR. UMESH VARMA's Sri Raja Rajeshwari Chest Clinic

Dr. Dr. Umesh Varma, a consummate and accomplished Pulmonologist, Chest & Sleep Specialist and an expert in Sleep disorder.

With more than 15 Years of experience in Clinical practice, Dr. Umesh varma- a founding Chairman of Sri Raja Rajeshwari Chest Clinic in Manikonda,Hyderabad has carved out a sterling niche for himself in Pulmonology and Respiratory Critical Care. He has acquired proficiency in diagnosing and treating with curative intent every nature of Respiratory problem including COPD (Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease), Multi Drug Resistant Tuberculosis (MDR and XDR TB), Asthma, Sleep Disorder, Interstitial Lung Disease and Occupational Lung Diseases, etc.

Dr. Umesh varma possesses considerable expertise in diagnosing and treating Respiratory diseases, Respiratory failure and other complicated chest infections, besides attending and rehabilitating critically ill cases both medical and surgical patients including post-operated and trauma-affected.

MBBS,MD,FABIP(AIMS KOCHI),FCCP(USA)
